Customer Survey

This survey will be used to measure the success of our recent project. Quality, timeliness and customer satisfaction will be assessed to ensure CWS standards have been met.

Your input is greatly valued, and we appreciate your time as we continually work to improve the CWS experience.

Instructions

Enter a rating (to the decimal) from 0-4.0 for each of the following categories.Your weighted average will be used to determine, in part, the success of the project.

Please consider the following evaluation scale:
  • 1.0 Deemed unacceptable
  • 2.0 Remedial action required
  • 3.0 Quality generally in accordance with standards, still room for improvement
  • 4.0 Meets or exceeds installation and quality standards
